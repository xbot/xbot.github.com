第四解：

A palindromic number reads the same both ways. The largest palindrome made from the product of two 2-digit numbers is 9009 = 91 × 99. Find the largest palindrome made from the product of two 3-digit numbers.

解：

{% codeblock lang:python %} largestPalindrome = 0 for i in range(100,1000): for j in range(100,1000): product = i * j if int(str(product)[::-1]) == product and product > largestPalindrome: largestPalindrome = product print largestPalindrome {% endcodeblock %}
